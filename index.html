About me

I'm an easygoing person with a big heart and a head for crunching numbers. Currently, I work in quantitative research at the Institute of Industrial Research, Stockholm. Regarding education, I have a Master's Degree in Economics and two bachelor's; one in Economics and one in Statistics. Hence, I have a solid quantitative profile, with particular emphasis on causal relationships.

I’m ambitious and determined, and always excited about problem-solving, coding, and uncovering data insights. Of course, I hope to bring excellency and some good vibes wherever I go.

I grew up in the countryside north of Stockholm, at the same farm where my father once grew up. I still consider that place to be a dear home, but at the same time, I'm excited to explore new places and gain new experiences and perspectives.

In my spare time, you'll find me volunteering for Amnesty International, dancing Bachata, or hanging out with friends and family.
